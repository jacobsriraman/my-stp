How it works

  1. Enter race distances and finish times.
  2. We standardize every performance to seconds per mile.
  3. A logarithmic best-fit line predicts training paces across effort durations.

Interpretation

What the fit means

The suggested paces are anchored to your requested effort durations, from an easy-limit effort of 100000 seconds down to a mile effort of 240 seconds.

This is still an estimate, not a replacement for a coach or context like terrain, weather, and fatigue.
